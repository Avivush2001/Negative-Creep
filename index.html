About Me

Negative Creep is a private tattoo studio in Eilat. I do custom tattos and from my own designs, tweaking them to the last excruciating detail and assuring you are satisfied. During tattoo sessions I will make sure you are as comfortable as possible. A good talk buddy and some snacks are included!

Currently I live in Be'er Sheva to study towards my arts degree, and thus commissions are closed till further notice.

Thank you to those who let me express my art with them!
